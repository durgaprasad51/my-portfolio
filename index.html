A little about me

Hello! I'm Durga Prasad — an AWS DevOps Engineer based in India with a deep-rooted passion for automation, cloud architecture and infrastructure reliability. Over 18+ years I've progressed from bare-metal hardware to orchestrating containerised workloads across multi-cloud environments.
