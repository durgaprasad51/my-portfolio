About Me

Hello — I'm Durga. Over the last 17+ years I've navigated diverse roles across IT infrastructure: site engineer at Team Computers, datacenter administrator, Windows system administrator at IBM India, and today an AWS DevOps engineer shipping cloud-native workloads.

That breadth means I understand the whole stack — hardware reality, networking, virtualization, identity, security, and clean automation. My pipelines rarely break, my infra is reproducible, and my migrations stay boring (which is exactly how they should be).
